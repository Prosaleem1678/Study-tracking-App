How to use

1. Open Daily Tracker and fill one row for each day.

2. The app saves everything in your browser using LocalStorage.

3. Weekly and Monthly summaries update automatically.

4. Use Setup to change targets and your study schedule.

Saved data notes

This page works offline after the first load. To move your data to another device, use browser export tools or copy the LocalStorage data from developer tools.
